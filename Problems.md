# Problem Set

## Overview

This document tracks weekly problem assignments for an on-site examination preparation course. Each week covers specific algorithmic topics with curated Easy and Medium difficulty problems from UVa Online Judge. Problems are designed for in-class practice and post-class reinforcement, building progressively toward midterm and final mock exams.
