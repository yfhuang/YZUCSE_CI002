# On-site Examination Policy

## Examination Duration and Format
- **Duration:** 60 minutes
- **Required Problems:** 2
- **Bonus Problems:** Up to 2 (extra credit)
- **Break:** None

## What You May Bring
- Valid student ID card (required for identity verification)
- One pen
- One sheet of A4 paper

## Prohibited Items
- Mobile phones, tablets, laptops, smartwatches, and other electronic devices (must be stored in bags, not on desk)
- AI tools, internet search, communication software, online coding platforms, or any unauthorized external assistance

## During the Exam
- Complete the exam independently
- No talking, communicating, borrowing items, or looking at another student's screen
- No leaving the room or using the restroom without prior instructor approval

## Exam Completion
- Submit work according to instructor's directions and within the allotted time
- Stop all writing, editing, running, and submission activities immediately when time is called

## Violations
Any violation of this policy may result in a score of zero and further disciplinary action in accordance with course and university regulations.
